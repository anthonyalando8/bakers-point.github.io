Placing Order

We take orders of over the phone with the following requirements:
  • Order Size: 1 Dozen items or 1 whole Quiche/Flan minimum
  • Arguello Order Date: At least 1 day in advance
  • McAllister Order Date: Same day orders accepted (call to confirm)
  • Flan orders require 48 hours notice
  • Desserts orders require 24 hours notice, pick up available Tuesday to Sun starting 9am at McAllister Location
  • All order times subject to availability
  • All order modifications must be done 24 hours in advance
We need the following to place your order:
  • Name for the Order
  • Desired Items
  • Pickup Date & Time
  • Credit Card Payment Details
